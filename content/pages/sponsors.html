Dinner with racing

In the Spring of each year, we recognize our sponsors and supporters at our Dinner with Racing. Those who enable us to do what we do are invited to join us on campus to celebrate our achievements for the year, network with our members, and receive thanks for the help they have given. We enjoy the opportunity to show our gratitude to all those who make our work possible and hope all our supporters join to share in the festivities.
